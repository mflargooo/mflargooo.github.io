Genodice

Genodice is rogue-like dice game where the player rolls a die on an isometric board to eliminate randomly spawned enemies. Clear the board of enemies to advance to the next board. The difference in the player's top facing number and the enemy's number is awared to player as poker chips. Purchase upgrades with them to grow stronger. Made for the 2022 GMTK Game Jam. Theme: Roll of the Dice.

The Horrors of Newell-Simon Hall

The Horrors of Newell-Simon Hall is a horror game centered around the roboceptionist, Marion "Tank" Lefleur, located in Carnegie Mellon University's Newell-Simon Hall. Navigate the dark Newell-Simon Hall atrium and repair the five power boxes. Beware of Tank who is roaming around in an attempt to keep the power off.

Dogwalker

The roles of dogwalking have been reversed: Your owner is chronically tied to their phone. You, the dog, are the only thing between your owner and danger! Tug on your leash to pull your hooman away from any and all harm. But watch where you step too! Made for the 2023 GTMK Game Jam. Theme: Roles Reversed.
